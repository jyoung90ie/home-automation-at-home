Smart Hub

Smart Hub helps you manage your Internet of Things (IoT) devices on your home network. It enables you to integrate devices from all supported protocols and brands - helping you take back control - your home, auomated.

To start using this application you will need to create an account, either by registering, or by logging in via an existing social account (e.g. Gmail). Then the fun begins!

What can Smart Hub do?

Good question! Once your logged in to an account you will have access to the following:

  • Devices
    • Add, Update, Delete, View
    • View and Export device logs
  • Device Locations: this is the ability to group devices by location, however, this is open to your own intrepretation. This will enable you interact with the group as opposed to individually selecting each individual device.
  • Events: Here you can create routines for your devices, which will involve triggers, notifications, and responses, building out your automation capability.
  • Notifications: you can specify the notification channels that should be used to contact you when any of your events have been triggered. At present you can be notified by email and mobile app
